The Japan Dietetic Association's Recommendation for Proteins

According to the Daily Reference Intakes (DRIs) established by the Japanese Dietetic Association (JDA), the ideal amount of proteins that have to be taken daily is located between 13% and 20% of the total amount of calories. According to the JDA, such an amount aims to prevent the progression of life-style related deseases.
